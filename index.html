About Me

Where Engineering
Meets Artistry

I believe architecture is the silent poetry of our built environment — every line drawn carries intention, every structure tells a story.

With over a decade of experience in architectural engineering, I specialize in creating spaces that harmonize human experience with environmental responsibility. My approach bridges the technical rigor of structural engineering with the refined sensibility of contemporary design.

From concept sketches to construction documentation, I guide projects through every phase with meticulous attention to detail, sustainable practices, and an unwavering commitment to aesthetic excellence.

"Architecture should speak of its time and place, but yearn for timelessness."

— My Design Philosophy
